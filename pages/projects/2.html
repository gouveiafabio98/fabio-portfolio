Crimes Exemplares (Multimedia)

Crimes Exemplares (Exemplary Crimes) is a literary work, composed of small texts, that report various crimes, in the most varied forms and situa-tions. Mostly surreal in nature, these criminal acts have impulses of extreme violence, without an ethical or moral nature, and, with its sort of comic speech, it enhances the absurd. Although it is impossible to calculate its veracity, these short stories challenge the reader to ask what is the limit of mental sanity and what can trigger the unreasonability of this type of acts.

In both the objects developed, these contrasting dualities were explored: the sobriety and the madness, the animal and the innocent, the veracity and the unbelief, supported by elements that create a connection to the years of the crimes.
